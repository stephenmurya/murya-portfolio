# Palmer Home Page Topology

Source URL: `https://palmer-template.framer.website/`

## Global shell
- Fixed top navigation with logo, quick links, and profile meta.
- Black site-wide background with white primary text and muted gray supporting text.
- Floating social button and floating "Use for Free" CTA on the right edge.

## Section order
1. Hero
   Large four-line headline, service tags bar, embedded media on the right, oversized "Akihiko™" wordmark.
2. About
   Left portrait image, large experience statement, contact pill, four capability labels, partner/client logo grid.
3. Featured Projects
   Intro copy + CTA, then staggered editorial project cards with strong negative space.
4. Capabilities
   Section intro followed by stacked numbered service rows.
5. Personal Profile
   Large sticky editorial text block with supporting descriptive paragraph.
6. Experience
   Practice / tenure timeline rows and studio history.
7. Testimonials
   Sticky review-focused section with large visual cards.
8. Awards
   Ranked awards list with image strips on the right.
9. Brand Partners
   Floating / layered brand visuals and cards.
10. Pricing
    Three pricing tiers with bullet-point inclusions.
11. Visual Journal
    Featured article strip plus editorial article cards.
12. Help Center
    FAQ-style numbered accordion list.
13. Final Section / Footer
    Closing statement, back-to-top CTA, quick links, network links, year mark, Framer badge.

## Responsive notes
- Desktop uses wide editorial spacing with sticky/fixed accents and asymmetrical card placement.
- Mobile collapses the layout into a narrow stacked column while retaining the same section order and dark theme.
- Breakpoints present in the extracted CSS:
  - `min-width: 1200px`
  - `810px - 1199.98px`
  - `max-width: 809.98px`
